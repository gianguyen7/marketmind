### Decisions
- **Regime-invariance discipline adopted as the primary research framing.** The earlier "find where the market is wrong and exploit it locally" was vulnerable to the devil's advocate critique that financial time series are non-stationary. Reframed to: produce structural, falsifiable hypotheses about *mechanisms* (not themes), and validate with walk-forward on post-freeze resolutions. See `docs/research_plan.md` new §Regime-Invariance Discipline. This supersedes the 2026-04-11 session 1 framing.
- **A2 mechanism REJECTED by pre-registered criteria.** Two falsification triggers fired: sign flip on `pct_lifetime_elapsed` and `log_volume` between train/val, plus secondary `price_vs_open` dominating all primaries. Root cause: train has only 2 markets — any feature is essentially market identity. Honest read: the split is underpowered, not the mechanism is wrong. Track A stops at A1; the mechanism defers to Track B5.
- **Track B1 scope trimmed after discovering existing code.** Original plan had 8 sub-steps; real gaps were only B1b (config), B1e (event grouping), B1f (CLI flags). B1c (categories.yaml), B1d (resolve_outcomes logging), and smoke tests deferred as unnecessary.
- **A2 population threshold changed from absolute ($100k) to empirical (bottom-25% volume).** Dry-run showed min volume at the $1M filter was $53M, making absolute dollar thresholds meaningless. Now defined as bottom quartile of fetched universe intersected with max days_to_end > 90.
- **`--gamma-fetch` does NOT re-run curated pipeline.** Caught mid-launch that the original code would have overwritten `data/processed/` which is frozen until Track B3. Patched to stop after Gamma step.
