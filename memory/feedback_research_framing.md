description: User wants ML to be the main contribution, not a benchmark comparison against the market

Don't position ML models as secondary benchmarks against Polymarket. The user wants this to be ML-driven research that contributes something novel.

**Why:** User rejected the initial plan that framed ML as "comparison benchmarks" and asked whether that was even novel (it's not). The project should use ML as the primary analytical tool, not just a yardstick.

**How to apply:** When designing experiments or writing analysis, lead with what ML reveals about market miscalibration, not with "market beats ML" findings. The market being good is the starting point, not the conclusion.
